🚫 What's wrong with pre-workout?

Ask any coach and they'll tell you the same thing. Pre-workout works — until it doesn't. The complaints are consistent:

  • ⚡Jitters — overstimulation that breaks concentration and interferes with technique
  • 📉The crash — a hard drop in energy mid-session or 2–3 hours after
  • 🧪Artificial ingredients — sweeteners, colourants, fillers you wouldn't eat any other way
  • 🔄Dependency — training without it starts to feel worse than it should

There's a point where you realise you're not using pre-workout for an edge — you're using it to feel normal. That's the moment most athletes start looking for an alternative.

Coffee has always been the world's most widely used performance enhancer. It just needed an upgrade.

Elite athletes are increasingly moving away from synthetic stimulants toward functional, daily protocols.

☕ What we looked for in a performance coffee

Not all performance coffees are created equal. Before testing, we set clear criteria. A product had to meet all of these to make our shortlist:

  • ☕Real coffee — freshly ground and roasted, not freeze-dried instant
  • 🌿Organic certification — clean sourcing, no fillers or maltodextrin
  • 🔬Transparent ingredients — exact doses listed, not proprietary blends hiding weak amounts
  • 🏃Athletic-specific formulation — Cordyceps as a primary ingredient for endurance and oxygen efficiency
