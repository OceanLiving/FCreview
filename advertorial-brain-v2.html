🧠 The problem with how most people approach brain health

As we get older — or simply work harder — cognitive health moves up the priority list. Focus, memory, processing speed, mental clarity under pressure.

The market offers plenty of solutions. But they all share the same problem:

  • 💊Supplement stacks — another set of pills to remember to take
  • 🧃Nootropic drinks — expensive, often artificial, another habit to build
  • 📋Cognitive protocols — high friction, require willpower to maintain
  • 🔄Dependency on new routines — most people stop within weeks

Research consistently shows that the most sustainable health interventions are ones that slot into existing behaviour. The habit with the best attendance record in most people's lives is coffee.
